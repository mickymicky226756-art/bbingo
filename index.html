Our Legacy (1879 – Present)
From California oil fields to global energy giant

Chevron's remarkable story began in 1879 with the founding of Pacific Coast Oil Company following the discovery of oil in California. Over 145 years of relentless innovation, strategic acquisitions, and pioneering exploration, Chevron has grown from a regional oil producer into one of the largest integrated energy corporations on Earth.

Today, Chevron operates across the entire hydrocarbon value chain — from upstream exploration and production, through midstream transportation and refining, to downstream chemicals and marketing — with world-class assets spanning six continents and generating revenues that consistently exceed $200 billion annually.

1879
Pacific Coast Oil Co. founded — first oil discovered in the fields of California, laying the foundation for what would become Chevron.
1936
Standard Oil of California rebranded — Chevron brand established, expanding operations across the Americas and Middle East.
2001
Merger with Texaco — a landmark $35 billion deal creating ChevronTexaco and dramatically expanding global reach and reserves.
2023
Record $35.5B net income — one of the strongest financial performances in Chevron's history, driven by Permian Basin production milestones.

How It Works

Complete guide to using the Chevron Investments platform

💳
Fund Your Account
How to Deposit
1
Open Deposit Page
Go to Home ➜ tap the Deposit button on the main screen.
2
Select Payment Method
Choose the bank or payment method you prefer (CBE, Telebirr, Abyssinia, etc.). The bank name and account number will be shown.
3
Transfer Money
Send the transfer to the provided bank account. Minimum amount is 900 ETB. Save your Transaction ID (TID).
4
Submit Transaction ID
Enter your Transaction Reference (TID/Txn ID) and amount, then tap Verify & Submit. After admin confirms, your balance will be credited.
Note: If you submit a correct TID, Admin will confirm within 24 hours. You cannot receive a deposit without entering a Transaction ID.
💸
Cash Out Your Earnings
How to Withdraw
1
Link Your Bank Account
Go to Profile ➜ Financial Routing ➜ link your preferred bank account. Withdrawal is not possible without this step.
2
Open Withdraw Page
Go to Home ➜ tap the Withdraw button. Your available balance will be displayed.
3
Enter Amount & Password
Minimum withdrawal is 270 ETB. Enter the amount you wish to withdraw, then enter your login password to confirm.
4
Request Sent
Tap Request Payout. Once admin confirms, the funds will be transferred to your linked account shortly.
Important: Withdrawal is only possible when you have an active investment plan. Requests without a linked bank account will not be accepted.
🎁
Bonus Codes
Promo Codes
What is it?
A promo code is a special code that lets you earn bonus funds. It can be given to you by Chevron's Telegram channel or team members.
How to Use?
Home ➜ Promo button ➜ enter the code in the Promo Code field ➜ tap Redeem. The bonus will be instantly credited to your balance.
Each promo code can only be used once. Expired or previously used codes will not work.
📋
Invitation Milestones
Task System
What is the Task System?
When you invite a certain number of valid members, you earn milestone bonuses. For example, invite 5 people and get a bonus, invite 10 and earn a bigger bonus.
How does it work?
Home ➜ Task ➜ Milestone Rewards list is shown. When your valid invite count reaches a milestone, the Claim button activates.
A valid invite means the invited person has purchased at least one investment plan. Registration alone is not counted.
📅
Daily Bonus
Daily Check-in
What is Check-in?
A daily mining bonus you can claim once per day. The reward starts at 3.8 ETB and may vary day to day.
How does it work?
Home ➜ Check-in ➜ tap "Claim ETB Now". The bonus will be instantly added to your balance. Don't forget every morning!
To earn check-in bonuses you may need an active investment plan (account type is upgraded). If only registered without investing, the amount may be lower.
🎡
Daily Prize Wheel
Lucky Spin
What is Spin?
You can spin the wheel once every 24 hours. You can win 5 ETB, 10 ETB, or Try Again.
How does it work?
Home ➜ Spin ➜ tap "SPIN NOW". When the wheel stops, your result will be shown. If you win a bonus, it is instantly credited to your balance.
🔒 Locked Prizes
Even if 500 ETB and 1000 ETB appear on the wheel, these are not winnable prizes — they cannot be landed on. They are decorative segments only.
If you spin today, you can only spin again tomorrow morning (you must wait 24 hours).
